Pizza Cupcakes

Like a mini pizza, except shaped like a cupcake

They're a great savory snack and a great addition to a game day buffet. Feel free to customize to your family's tastes with additions like ham, sausage, bell peppers, salami, mushrooms, olives, jalapenos, etc. I recommend not omitting the tomato, that really adds something special.

Ingredients

  1. cooking spray
  2. 8 medium cherry tomatoes
  3. 1 (11 ounce) can refrigerated pizza crust
  4. 8 teaspoons shredded mozzarella cheese
  5. 1/2 cup pizza sauce (such as Classico®)
  6. 8 teaspoons finely chopped red onion
  7. ½ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  8. 8 slices pepperoni, quartered
  9. Italian seasoning to taste
  10. red pepper flakes to taste
  11. garlic salt to taste
